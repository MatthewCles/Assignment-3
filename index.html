Bubble Wars!!

How it Works:

At the start, there are 14 individually colored bases. These bases can spawn minions to attack other bases or capture command points that will spawn minions for whatever color controls it. Minions can group up with minions of the same color, attack other minions, flee from other minions, attack enemy bases, capture command points, attack command points, repair its colors base, and repair its colors command points. The last color standing wins...

Until you click on "New Game!"

Now featuring Save and Load functionallity! Save a game and reload it to see if the outcome will change!

Can you save and load at the perfect times to guide the results you want? or was your favorite color indeed doomed from the start?!

Enjoy!
